Crafted from scraps, not screens.

Each Raw Cut is a one-of-a-kind character built by hand from paper, cardboard, tape, and whatever scraps were within reach.

No algorithms decided their faces. No AI rendered their armor. Every imperfect crease, every torn edge, every crooked eye — that's the point.

Imperfection is the signature. In a world of pixel-perfect generative art, Raw Cuts chooses the rough, the real, the handmade.
